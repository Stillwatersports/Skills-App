The Process

Simple from start
to splash.

01
Book Online
Fill out the booking form below. Tell us your location, group size, skill level, and preferred dates. We'll confirm within 24 hours.
02
We Plan Your Session
Christian will reach out to discuss your goals, pick the best spot on the water, and tailor the lesson plan to your group.
03
We Come to You
We arrive with everything — boat, gear, and a structured plan. All you need to bring is sunscreen and enthusiasm.
04
Learn & Progress
Get on the water, build skills, and have a blast. Students get access to the Stillwater Skills app to track progress all season long.
