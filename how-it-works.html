03
Session Day

We come to your lake.
Fully set up.

On the day of your session Christian arrives at your lake with everything — the Sanger DLX boat, all equipment, life jackets, and a structured plan ready to go. You don't need to bring a single thing except yourselves.

What to bring: Swimwear, sunscreen, water, snacks, and a towel. Everything else is provided.

04
On the Water

Learn, progress,
have fun.

Every session follows a structured progression — starting with safety and fundamentals, building through skills at the right pace for each rider. Our progressive system is designed so that everyone gets up on their first session.

After your session, all Stillwater Sports students get access to the Stillwater Skills app — track your progress, earn Bronze through Platinum badges, and pick up exactly where you left off next time.

Common Questions

Before you book.

Do I need my own boat or lake access?
No — we bring the boat and travel to you. You just need a lake. Distance surcharges apply for locations 1.5+ hrs from Manotick.
What age can kids start?
Kids 6 and older are welcome. Children under 16 must be with a parent or guardian. We have equipment in all sizes.
What if the weather is bad?
Light rain — we go. Heavy rain or thunder — we reschedule with full credit. Full refund if no date is available before September 30.
What is the deposit policy?
30% deposit required. Non-refundable. Full payment due 48 hours before session via Square payment link. See our refund policy for full details.
