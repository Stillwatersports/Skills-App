The Building Blocks

Pass, Set,
Block.

01

Pass

A single run behind the boat — typically 3 to 4 minutes. A complete turn is made up of 6–8 passes, giving each rider a chance to practice, rest, and go again before wrapping up their set.

02

Set

One person's full turn on the water — 20 to 30 minutes including occasional stops for coaching notes. Each set is 6–8 passes. Most participants do 1–2 sets per block depending on group size and energy level.

03

Block

Morning, Afternoon, or Evening — each block is 4 hours. We fit 2–3 sets per hour. A full day uses any 2 of the 3 blocks (e.g. morning + afternoon with a lunch break, or morning + evening for the best flat water). In a 6-person group, everyone gets roughly 2 sets per block.

Planning Your Group

What's the right group size?

Ideal group sizes are 4–8 people per block. That gives everyone enough time on the water while keeping the energy up on the boat. For a full day (2 blocks), a group of 6 would each get around 2 sets in the morning and 2 in the afternoon.

Smaller group? No problem. If you're just 1–3 people, contact us, check out our Come to Us pricing, or we can adjust the timing to fit your group. If you want to learn, we will make it happen.
