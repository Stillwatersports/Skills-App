The Boat

Sanger DLX
Built for All Sports.

The Sanger DLX is one of the most respected boats in competitive water sports — its hull design specifically engineeredfor optimum wake shape, and performance at both ski and wakeboard speeds. This isn't a recreational runabout. It's a purpose-built coaching platform.

The hybrid design means it performs across every discipline — clean, flat wakes for slalom and barefoot, configurable wave for wakeboard and wakesurf, and stable, predictable handling that lets the driver focus entirely on the rider.

Boat Features

Fully loaded.
Competition spec.

⚡

Perfect Pass

GPS-based speed control system used in sanctioned competition. Locks speed precisely regardless of rider weight, rope pull, or water conditions. Essential for serious training.

🗼

Wakeboard Tower

Full wakeboard tower for elevated rope attachment — creates a higher pull angle making easier starts for beginners and for bigger air on expert wakeboard tricks and grabs.

🦅

The Boom

The bar extending out the drivers side allows our coach to work directly alongside beginner riders at low speed. More stable than a long rope and positioned so the coach can give real time corrections and encouragement.

🌊

Surf Tabs

Adjustable wake shaping tabs for wakesurf sessions. Dial in the exact wave shape and size — from mellow and forgiving for beginners to steep and powerful for advanced surfers.

💧

Ballast Bags

Weighted ballast bags to increase displacement and create a larger, more pronounced wake for wakeboarding and wakesurfing. Fill or empty based on rider preference and discipline.

☂️

Bimini Top

Full bimini top for shade and sun protection during long days on the water. Important for families, all-day sessions, and keeping everyone comfortable.
