The Boat

Sanger DLX.

Sanger boats are one of the most respected manufacturers in competitive water sports — The DLX specifically chosen for its hull design, wake shape, and performance at both ski and wakeboard speeds. This isn't a recreational runabout. It's a purpose-built all-watersports platform. The open bow design allows up to 8 passengers comfortably!

The hull design means the boat performs across every discipline — clean, flat wakes for slalom and barefoot, configurable wave for wakeboard and wakesurf, and stable, predictable handling that lets the driver focus entirely on the rider.

Boat Features

Competition spec.

Perfect Pass

GPS-based speed control system used in sanctioned competition. Locks speed precisely regardless of rider weight, rope pull, or water conditions. Essential for serious training and beginner learning.

Wakeboard Tower

Full wakeboard tower for elevated rope attachment — creates a higher pull angle for easier starts and bigger air.

The Boom

A solid bar extending out the driver's side of the boat, The Barefoot Boom was originally designed to create a more stable pull in flatter water beside the boat for early barefoot water-skiers. This quickly evolved into an excellent teaching tool for new waterskiers and wakeboarders. It allows the coach to work directly alongside the rider at low speed from the driver's seat. Critical for giving real time instructions and corrections. It is height adjustable for all ages and skill levels. Even the best waterskier in the world still hones their skills on the boom!

Surf Tabs

Adjustable wake shaping tabs for wakesurf sessions. Dial in the exact wave shape and size — from mellow and forgiving for beginners to steep and powerful for advanced surfers.

Ballast Bags

Weighted ballast bags to increase displacement and create a larger, more pronounced wake for wakeboarding and wakesurfing.

Super Fly High

The Super Fly High extends the height of the wakeboard tower, primarily used for barefooting. The higher pull point allows for easier and faster progression of barefoot tricks.

️

Bimini Top

Full bimini top for shade and sun protection during long days on the water. Important for families, all-day sessions, and keeping the coach and observers comfortable.
