Features

Everything a coach
and athlete needs.

🏅

Bronze → Platinum Progression

Structured skill tiers that lock until you complete the level below. Real learning paths — not cherry-picked tricks.

✅

Coach Approval Queue

Athletes submit skills for approval. Coaches review, approve or reject with notes — all from the app. No paper needed.

🎬

Video Submission

Athletes attach a video when submitting a skill. Coaches review footage before approving. Evidence-based progression.

📍

GPS Session Tracking

Track runs, distance, and top speed every session. Build a full history of time on the water over the season.

🏆

Leaderboard & Social

Club leaderboards, friend connections, and shareable skill profiles. Friendly competition drives motivation.

🛡️

Safety Certifications

Built-in dryland safety quiz, driver certification, and advanced safety modules. Unlock water skills only after safety is confirmed.

👥

Club & Class Management

Admins manage rosters, coaches manage classes, athletes track their own progress. Scales from a single coach to a full club.

📋

Printable Checklists

Generate a printable dock checklist for paper-based sessions. Perfect for coaching without phones on the water.

📱

No App Store Required

Works on any phone as a Progressive Web App. Add to your home screen — no download, no App Store, no updates to manage.
