Christian Richards has spent twenty-five years on the water — competing, coaching, and teaching first-timers how to ride.

He's a top-100 worldwide barefoot athlete, a certified WSWC instructor, and has coached thousands of new waterskiers and wakeboarders across Ontario. He knows exactly how to read a beginner — the body position, the rope tension, the boat speed — and adjust everything so you stand up the first time.

Good to Know
What if it rains? For normal lessons, light rain is OK, and the coach has final say on weather conditions. However, we want your date night to be perfect so we will accept postponements at your request. Heavy rain, thunder, or unsafe wind = we reschedule, no penalty.
First time skiing?Perfect. We have taught hundreds of first-timers, if not over a thousand. We'll start with basics, take it slow, and build confidence.
Dietary restrictions?Mention them when you book — all three dinner options can accommodate vegetarian, gluten-free, or other needs with notice.
Special occasion?Engagement, anniversary, birthday — let us know. We can help add a personal touch (flowers, a card, a playlist)
Why does Friday or Saturday cost more?Friday and Saturday are peak demand evenings. Bookings on those nights are +$50 flat. Sunday through Thursday are full price as listed on each dinner card.
Location? Various spots along the Rideau River, Manotick, Riverside south, Barrhaven, Kemptville, Kars and more have great staging areas depending on your package. Address and dock details sent on confirmation.
