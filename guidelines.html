Please read through the following requirements before booking your session. They cover everything you need to know about location access, participant rules, what to bring, safety expectations, and booking policies. If you have any questions, contact us before booking — we're happy to help.

01

Lake & Location Requirements

Boat Launch

  • A usable boat launch must be present on the same body of water as your session
  • A trailer-friendly ramp (concrete or gravel) is strongly preferred — we arrive with a full-size ski boat and trailer
  • Minimum 8 feet of clearance required for any culverts, bridges, or locks on the route to the launch
  • If the launch is on a different lake or waterway, please advise us before booking

Water Requirements

  • Any lake, river, or waterway that allows motorized watercraft is suitable
  • Open water preferred — a clear stretch of at least 200–300m is ideal for most disciplines
  • We also operate on the Rideau River in Ottawa — no cottage required
  • Public parks and boat launches are acceptable if no private cottage access is available

Location

  • Please ensure there is a bathroom on-site or nearby
  • A covered area and/or seating is recommended for observers
  • A nearby gas station (non-marine) is strongly preferred for full day and multi-day bookings

02

Participant Requirements

Age

  • All ages welcome — our youngest skier was 2.5 years old, our oldest is… undisclosed!
  • Children under 16 must have a parent or guardian present on shore at all times
  • If a minor's parents are not present, contact information and additional written consent is required prior to the session — a form will be provided
  • Equipment is available in all sizes — we accommodate all ages
  • We offer lifejackets in every size but recommend bringing your own for proper fitment
    • A well-fitting lifejacket should be snug but not tight. To test: lift the jacket by the shoulders — if it rises to or past the wearer's ears, it does not fit properly.

Health & Physical Condition

  • Participants should be comfortable in the water — basic swimming ability is required
  • If any participant has a physical limitation, injury, or medical condition, please advise us in advance
  • Participants who appear to be under the influence of alcohol or drugs will not be permitted to participate — no refund will be issued
  • Alcohol and drugs are not permitted on the boat

Waivers

  • A signed liability waiver is required for every participant before entering the water
  • Waivers for minors must be signed by a parent or legal guardian
  • Waivers will be sent upon booking confirmation — please complete them in advance
  • Our full waiver and policies are available at learnwatersports.ca/policies

What to Bring

We provide everything on the water. Just show up ready to ride.

Required

  • Sunscreen — apply before boarding

Optional but Recommended

04

On-Water Safety Rules

Safety is covered at the start of every session. These rules are non-negotiable and apply to all participants on and around the water.

Life Jackets

  • A properly fitting PFD must be worn by every rider while in the water — no exceptions
  • Children under 16 must wear a PFD on the boat as well

Spotter / Observer

  • A designated spotter must face the rider at all times while they are in the water
  • Hand signals are reviewed at the start of each session: thumb up, thumb down, OK, stop, emergency

In the Water

  • Always hold your ski(s) or board up out of the water after a fall — signals your location to the driver and other boaters
  • Stay visible — do not swim toward the boat until instructed by the coach
  • After a fall, give the appropriate signal — OK or NOT OK. The boat will return slowly to pick you up
  • Never approach the boat near the propeller — wait for the all-clear signal

On the Boat

  • Remain seated while the boat is at speed
  • No standing or sitting on the bow, gunwales, or tower while underway
  • Participants who have consumed alcohol will not be permitted to ride — no refund will be issued
  • All instructions from the coach must be followed at all times on and around the water
Coach Authority: The coach has final say on all safety decisions — including weather, conditions, and participant readiness. Sessions may be paused or ended at any time if safety is a concern.
05

Booking & Payment Requirements

For full policy details see learnwatersports.ca/policies

To Secure Your Booking

  • A 30% deposit is required — your date is not held until the deposit is received
  • Full payment is due 48 hours before your session via Square payment link
  • Failure to pay by this deadline may result in forfeiture of the booking without refund of deposit
  • Bookings are confirmed only upon receipt of deposit and written confirmation from Stillwater Sports

Cancellation Policy

  • Deposit is non-refundable in all cases of client cancellation
  • Cancellation by client — no refund, no credit
  • Cancellation by Stillwater Sports — reschedule at no charge with credit for any money paid. Full refund if no suitable date is available
  • Cancellations must be submitted by email to bookings@learnwatersports.ca — not accepted by phone or text

Weather Policy

  • Light rain — session proceeds as scheduled
  • Heavy rain or thunder — session cancelled by coach, full credit to rebook
  • Overcast or wind only — session proceeds; if conditions deteriorate, pro-rated credit for remaining time
  • If no suitable rebook date before September 30 — full refund of all payments made
  • The coach has final say on all weather-related decisions

Surcharges

  • Tubing: +$30/hr additional fuel surcharge — waived for 2+ consecutive day bookings. It's a fun way to break up the other activities!
  • Distance: +$100 flat (1.5–3hr) or +$200 flat (3–6hr) from Ottawa

Stillwater Skills App

All Stillwater Sports students receive free access to the Stillwater Skills app — a structured progression platform for tracking skills and earning certifications from Bronze through Platinum.

  • Access at learnwatersports.ca/stillwaterapp — free for all students
  • Track your skills from Bronze through Gold — complete Gold to unlock the secret Platinum level
  • Submit videos for coach approval and sign-off on each skill
  • GPS session tracking — record your time on the water all season and compare against your friends
  • We recommend creating your account before your session — we'll sign off your first skills on the day
