  • If a minor's parents are not present, contact information and additional written consent is required prior to the session — a form will be provided
  • We offer lifejackets in every size but recommend bringing your own for proper fitment
    • A well-fitting lifejacket should be snug but not tight. To test: lift the jacket by the shoulders — if it rises to or past the wearer's ears, it does not fit properly.

Health & Physical Condition

  • A signed liability waiver is required for every participant before entering the water
  • Waivers will be sent upon booking confirmation — please complete them in advance
  • Our full waiver and policies are available at learnwatersports.ca/policies
03

We provide everything on the water. Just show up ready to ride.

Required

  • Swimwear
  • Sunscreen — apply before boarding
  • Water & snacks for your group
  • Towels
  • Signed waivers (sent after booking)

Optional but Recommended

  • Your own life jacket — we have all sizes but yours is better
  • Your own ski, board, or handle
  • Rash guard or UV protection
  • Warm layer for morning/evening sessions
  • Camera / GoPro
  • Change of clothes for the drive home
We Provide Everything Else
Sanger DLX boat · Fuel · Waterskis · Wakeboards · Wakesurf board · Combo skis · Trick skis · Slalom ski · Ropes · Handles · PFDs in all sizes · Barefoot boom · Wakeboard tower · Surf tabs · Ballast bags · First aid kit · Safety equipment
04

  • Participants who have consumed alcohol will not be permitted to ride — no refund will be issued
Coach Authority: The coach has final say on all safety decisions — including weather, conditions, and participant readiness. Sessions may be paused or ended at any time if safety is a concern.
05

For full policy details see learnwatersports.ca/policies

  • Cancellation by client — no refund, no credit
  • Cancellations must be submitted by email to bookings@learnwatersports.ca — not accepted by phone or text

  • Light rain — session proceeds as scheduled
  • Heavy rain or thunder — session cancelled by coach, full credit to rebook
  • Overcast or wind only — session proceeds; if conditions deteriorate, pro-rated credit for remaining time
  • If no suitable rebook date before September 30 — full refund of all payments made
  • The coach has final say on all weather-related decisions

  • Tubing: +$30/hr additional fuel surcharge — waived for 2+ consecutive day bookings. It's a fun way to break up the other activities!
  • Distance: +$100 flat (1.5–3hr) or +$200 flat (3–6hr) from Ottawa
06

All Stillwater Sports students receive free access to the Stillwater Skills app — a structured progression platform for tracking skills and earning certifications from Bronze through Platinum.

  • Access at learnwatersports.ca/stillwaterapp — free for all students
  • GPS session tracking — record your time on the water all season and compare against your friends
  • We recommend creating your account before your session — we'll sign off your first skills on the day
