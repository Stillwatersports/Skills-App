Privacy Policy

Your Privacy

Last updated: April 2026

Stillwater Sports ("we", "our", "us") is operated by Christian Richards, based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Ontario privacy law.

What We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number provided through our booking form
  • Location information (lake or cottage area) provided for session planning
  • Session preferences (group size, skill level, sport) provided at booking
  • For the Stillwater Skills app: username, optional email, skill progress data, and GPS session data

How We Use It

  • To confirm and manage your booking
  • To communicate session details, reminders, and follow-ups
  • To operate and improve the Stillwater Skills app
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing

Third Party Services

We use the following services to operate our platform:

  • Supabase — database and authentication for booking and app data (supabase.com)
  • Netlify — website hosting (netlify.com)
  • Formspree — booking form submission processing (formspree.io)
  • Square — payment processing for deposits (squareup.com)

Each of these services maintains their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. Contact us at privacy@learnwatersports.ca.

Terms of Service

Last updated: April 2026

By booking a session with Stillwater Sports, using our website, or using the Stillwater Skills app, you agree to these terms.

Bookings

  • A 30% deposit is required to secure a booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of the session.
  • Bookings are confirmed only upon receipt of the deposit and written confirmation from Christian Richards.
  • We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule sessions due to unsafe weather, water, or other conditions. In such cases, a full credit or rescheduled date will be offered.

Conduct & Safety

  • All participants must follow the coach's instructions at all times on and around the water.
  • Life jackets are required for all participants under 16 and strongly recommended for all others.
  • Participants under the influence of alcohol or drugs will not be permitted to participate and no refund will be issued.
  • Stillwater Sports reserves the right to end a session early if participant conduct or safety conditions warrant it.

Age & Health

  • Children aged 6 and older may participate. Children under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.
  • Participants with heart conditions, back injuries, or other relevant medical conditions should consult a doctor before participating.

Refund Policy

Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Cancellation by Client

  • More than 7 days before session: Full deposit refund
  • 3–7 days before session: 50% of deposit refunded. Remaining credit valid for rebooking within the same season.
  • Less than 72 hours before session: Deposit is non-refundable. Credit may be issued at coach's discretion for genuine emergencies.
  • Same day cancellation: No refund. No credit.

Cancellation by Stillwater Sports

  • If we cancel due to weather, unsafe conditions, or coach illness, you will receive a full refund of any deposit paid, or the option to reschedule at no additional charge.
  • We will notify you as early as possible in the event of a cancellation.

How to Cancel

To cancel or reschedule your booking, email bookings@learnwatersports.ca with your name and booking date.
